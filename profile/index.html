Hyunsik Min

M.S. Candidate in Future Convergence Technology

I am an M.S. student at Soonchunhyang University and a graduate researcher in the Smart Autonomous & Infrastructure Lab (SAIL), advised by Byeongjoon Noh. My work focuses on models that move beyond benchmarks and remain useful in practical settings.

Recent work spans trajectory prediction for autonomous driving, photovoltaic power forecasting, legal AI, and foundation-model research for interpretable and trustworthy decision support.
