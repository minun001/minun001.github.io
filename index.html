Overview

Practical AI across mobility, energy, and decision support

I focus on models that stay useful outside controlled benchmarks, from road-behavior prediction to energy forecasting and language-based decision support.

Mobility intelligence

I study autonomous driving and traffic analysis with an emphasis on early motion understanding, road context, and safer real-world operation.

Energy forecasting

I work on photovoltaic forecasting and scalable energy management with time-series models tied to operational conditions that matter in practice.

LLM applications

I use language models as structured reasoning tools in legal and safety-oriented settings where traceability matters as much as predictive strength.
